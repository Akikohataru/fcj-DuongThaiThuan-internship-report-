### Week 2 Goals:

- Master foundational AWS services for SmartMenu development.
- Practice access control and networking on AWS.
- Understand how to deploy and manage compute resources on AWS.
- Prepare knowledge for Serverless system development.

### Achievements During Week 2:

- Understood how to manage users, groups, and authorization with AWS IAM.
- Grasped basic networking architecture on Amazon VPC, including VPC, Subnets, Tables, and Security Groups.
- Successfully completed the launch and management of Amazon EC2 Instances.
- Understood the process of deploying and managing databases on Amazon RDS.
- Got familiar with Amazon DynamoDB and the NoSQL database model.
- Established a foundation to deploy Serverless services for the SmartMenu system in the upcoming weeks.
